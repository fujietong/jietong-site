Our Duo

Welcome to our Duo page! We are Dr. Jietong Fu, tenor, and Dr. Xin Wu, pianist, a husband-and-wife team specializing in opera, art song, and collaborative performance. Together, we bring poetry, passion, and artistry to every stage. We are available for concerts, recitals, masterclasses, and special events.

Meet Pianist – Dr. Xin Wu

Dr. Xin Wu is a pianist specializing in opera, vocal coaching, and collaborative performance. She serves as an accompanist and vocal coach at Morgan State University and a staff pianist at the Peabody Conservatory and Preparatory, where she works closely with singers to prepare for recitals, competitions, and professional engagements. She is also a vocal coach at the Charm City Opera Retreat. With extensive experience in vocal collaboration, Dr. Wu has played for numerous opera productions, and she frequently collaborates in major vocal competitions, including NATS, NATSAA, the Hong Kong International Vocal Competition, and the Met Laffont Competition. She recently released an album with The Cecilia Collective titled The Forgotten Genius of Josephine Lang, highlighting the work of this remarkable composer.

Featured Programs

A Tapestry of Emotions

A musical journey not one of distance, but of emotion. Like a traveler setting out under the quiet veil of night, begin with anticipation, stepping gently into the unknown. Each piece in this program is a moment on the path — of sorrow and joy, longing and tenderness, passion and peace.

Echoes of Ancient Verses

This program presents a rich tapestry of Chinese art songs, all set to the timeless words of classical Chinese poetry. The selections span a wide range of musical styles — from ancient melodies and traditional settings, to works by modern and contemporary composers, as well as neo-classical creations and popular adaptations. Together, they offer a journey through centuries of poetic expression, reimagined through music.
